How to Reach Us

01

✈️ Fly to Srinagar

Sheikh ul-Alam International Airport (SXR) in Srinagar is the gateway to Gulmarg. Direct flights operate from Delhi, Mumbai, Bangalore, Kolkata, Jammu and other major Indian cities. Flight time from Delhi is approximately 1 hour 15 minutes.

02

🚗 Airport Pickup

We arrange direct pickup from Srinagar Airport for all guests. Share your flight number when booking and our driver will be at arrivals when you land. The drive from Srinagar Airport to Gulmarg takes approximately 1.5 to 2 hours depending on conditions.

03

🏔 Arrive in Gulmarg

Gulmarg sits at 2,650m in the Pir Panjal range, 56km from Srinagar city centre. The final stretch of road from Tangmarg to Gulmarg (13km) climbs steeply. Private vehicles and taxis are available from Tangmarg if needed.

04

🎿 You're Ready

Once at your hotel, our team checks in with you to confirm your session schedule, equipment needs and any logistics for the following morning. First lesson briefings typically happen over dinner on arrival day.

Important Information

India Formalities

🛂 Visa Requirements

Most international visitors require an Indian visa. The e-Visa (eTV) is available for citizens of most countries and can be obtained online through the Indian government portal before travel. Apply at least 4–7 days in advance. Visa on arrival is available for select nationalities.

Indian nationals do not require any special permits to visit Gulmarg or the broader Kashmir Valley (Srinagar, Gulmarg, Pahalgam, Sonamarg).

📋 Protected Area Permits

  • No Inner Line Permit (ILP) required for Gulmarg, Srinagar, Pahalgam or Sonamarg for Indian or most foreign nationals
  • Certain border areas beyond Gulmarg may require special permits — our team advises on a case-by-case basis
  • Passport/ID required at all hotel check-ins and some gondola operations
  • Registration with local police may be required for extended stays — we handle this for all guests

🏥 Travel Insurance

We strongly recommend comprehensive travel insurance that covers mountain activities including skiing and off-piste adventure sports. Verify that your policy includes emergency mountain rescue and medical evacuation — standard travel insurance often excludes these. Several specialist mountain sports insurers offer suitable policies.

🌡 Health & Altitude

  • Gulmarg is at 2,650m — most guests adapt quickly without issues
  • Gondola Phase 2 reaches 3,980m — take Day 1 slowly at this altitude
  • Drink 3+ litres of water daily on the mountain
  • Avoid alcohol the night before high-altitude sessions
  • Symptoms of altitude sickness: headache, nausea, fatigue — descend and rest if these appear
  • No vaccinations specifically required for Kashmir travel
On the Ground

Getting Around Kashmir

🚐 Airport → Gulmarg Transfer

We provide private car transfer from Srinagar Airport directly to your Gulmarg hotel. This is the most comfortable and efficient way to arrive — no negotiating with taxi drivers, no confusion about routes. Book this when you reserve your session with us.

🚕 Local Taxis

Yellow-top taxis are the main form of local transport in Kashmir. Fixed government rates apply for major routes including Srinagar–Gulmarg. Private cabs can be hired for the day at reasonable rates. Our team pre-arranges all transport for guests so nothing needs to be figured out on arrival.

📱 Connectivity

Mobile coverage in Gulmarg is available on all major Indian networks (Airtel, Jio, BSNL). 4G is available in the main meadow area. Signal drops significantly on the upper mountain above the gondola base — download offline maps before heading up. WhatsApp and standard calling work reliably in the village.

💰 Money & Payments

ATMs are available in Gulmarg town and more reliably in Tangmarg and Srinagar. Carry sufficient cash for gondola tickets, local restaurants and equipment rental as many operators are cash-only. Major hotels and our own booking system accept card and UPI payments.
