⭐ Asia's 5th Best Ski Destination

Gulmarg

The "Meadow of Flowers" and Asia's premier ski destination. Home to the world's highest gondola at 3,980m, legendary deep powder slopes and breathtaking alpine scenery in every season.

  • Gulmarg Gondola — Asia's highest cable car reaching 3,980m
  • World-class skiing Dec–Apr across 4 terrain zones
  • Wildflower meadows, trekking and camping in summer
  • Asia's highest golf course + historic St. Mary's Church (1895)
  • Beginner poma slopes to expert backcountry for all levels
