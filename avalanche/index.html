Before You Go Off-Piste

📋

Read the Daily Forecast

The Gulmarg Avalanche Center advisory is updated every morning at 8:00 AM. Check it before every single off-piste session — conditions change significantly overnight.

👤

Hire a Certified Guide

Our guides hold AIARE Level 1 and New Zealand avalanche certifications. They carry full safety equipment and conduct terrain assessments before committing to any line.

📡

Carry a Transceiver

Avalanche transceiver, probe and shovel are mandatory for all off-piste travel. Available for hire from our ski shop. Know how to use them before you need them.

👫

Never Ski Alone

Off-piste skiing at Gulmarg must always be done in a group of at least two people with compatible transceivers. Solo off-piste travel is not permitted with our guides.

🗣

Tell Someone Your Plan

Before heading off-piste, tell a contact at the gondola base your intended line and expected return time. Share the rescue grid map reference for your area.

🎓

Take an Avalanche Course

The Gulmarg Avalanche Center offers free weekly awareness sessions and formal courses during the ski season. If you haven't taken one, we strongly encourage it.
