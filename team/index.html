Our Standards

What Every Guide Brings

📜

J&K Tourism Licensed

Every guide holds an official licence from the Jammu & Kashmir Himalayan Tourism Department — the required professional credential for all mountain guiding in the region.

⚠️

Avalanche Safety Trained

Several team members hold AIARE Level 1 certifications and have completed international training with New Zealand mountain guides. No off-piste session goes without trained safety personnel.

🏠

Born in Gulmarg

Every instructor grew up in Gulmarg or the surrounding villages. Their knowledge of this mountain isn't academic — it's lived across generations.

🎯

Level-Matched Teaching

Every student is assessed at the start of their first session and matched to the right programme, instructor and terrain. No generic group approach.

🤝

Guest-First Always

Safety, pacing and enjoyment take priority over pushing terrain or speed. A confident, happy guest is the only measure of a successful session.

🌿

Mountain Respect

Gulmarg is a protected biosphere reserve. Our team operates with full environmental awareness and strict adherence to designated guiding areas.
