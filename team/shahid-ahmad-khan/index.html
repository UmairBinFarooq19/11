About Shahid

Shahid Ahmad Khan found his calling on a snowboard at a young age, developing the kind of technical fluency that only comes from years of daily riding across varied mountain conditions. He has spent his entire professional career in Gulmarg, building his teaching practice season by season on the slopes he knows intimately.

His value as a snowboard instructor lies in his ability to break the sport's technique into understandable progressions. Snowboarding has a notoriously steep initial learning curve, and Shahid's approach — patient, structured and adapted to each individual rider — significantly shortens the time between first-day struggles and genuine enjoyment on snow.

Teaching Approach

Shahid works with riders of all ages and experience levels, from those stepping onto a board for the very first time to more experienced snowboarders wanting to improve their carving, powder technique or introduction to off-piste terrain. He is particularly effective with younger students, whose confidence he builds gradually through positive reinforcement and carefully selected terrain progressions.

He also teaches skiing and holds the same guide licence as all members of our team, allowing him to accompany guests across the full range of Gulmarg's terrain when conditions call for it.
