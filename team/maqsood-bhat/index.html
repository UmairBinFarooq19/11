About Maqsood Bhat

Maqsood Bhat is a lifelong Gulmarg resident with a deep personal relationship with the mountains he grew up on. His years on these slopes have given him an instinctive understanding of the terrain, seasonal snow patterns and the best routes for guests of every ability level.

As an instructor, Maqsood combines technical ski knowledge with the kind of genuine local warmth that makes a trip to Gulmarg feel personal rather than transactional. He adapts his teaching style naturally to each student — structured and methodical for those who want a clear progression, relaxed and exploratory for those who simply want to enjoy the mountain.

Qualifications & Certifications

  • Certified ski instructor — all ability levels
  • Official Guide Licence — J&K Himalayan Tourism Department
  • Backcountry terrain experience across the full Apharwat range
  • Avalanche awareness training
  • 12+ seasons of continuous professional guiding
