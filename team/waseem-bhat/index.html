About Waseem Bhat

Waseem Bhat was born and raised in Mul Bangil, a village in the Tangmarg area at the foot of the mountains that would define his life. From an early age he took to the slopes with a natural instinct and an appetite for speed that earned him the nickname "The Tiger" among fellow skiers and the community that watched him develop.

His career as a competitive athlete took him to national-level championships where he represented India four times — a rare achievement demanding both exceptional technical ability and unwavering physical conditioning. As a coach, he brings the same focused, technical approach to his teaching across all levels — from beginners to advanced skiers wanting to push into demanding terrain.

Qualifications & Certifications

  • India National Ski Team representative — four occasions
  • Certified ski instructor, all ability levels
  • Official Guide Licence — J&K Himalayan Tourism Department
  • Off-piste and advanced terrain experience across the full Gulmarg terrain map
